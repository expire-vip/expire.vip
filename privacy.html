Privacy Policy

Expire.vip

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Expire.vip. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. Expire.vip is a "serverless" frontend application. We do not have backend databases, and we do not store, read, or sell your personal data.

2. What Information We Access

When you sign in with Google, we request access to the following information via Google APIs:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your email address to authenticate your session.
  • Google Drive AppData (drive.appdata): We use a hidden, secure folder in your Google Drive to save your list of expiring items. This allows you to sync your items across multiple devices.
  • Google Calendar Events (calendar.events): We request access to create, view, and delete calendar events specifically created by Expire.vip to provide you with automated expiration reminders.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the requested information exclusively to provide the core functionality of the app. Specifically, to securely store your expiration items in your personal Google Drive and to insert reminder events into your Google Calendar so you do not miss expiration dates.

4. Data Sharing, Transfer, and Disclosure

We do not share, transfer, or disclose your Google user data with anyone.

  • No Third Parties: Expire.vip does not share, transfer, or disclose your Google user data (including Calendar and Drive data) to any third parties, business partners, or affiliates.
  • No External Servers: Because Expire.vip operates entirely within your web browser, your data flows directly between your device and Google's servers. We do not intercept, transfer, or host your data on our own servers.
  • No Advertising or Sales: We explicitly do not sell your data, nor do we use or disclose your Google user data for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Data Protection Mechanisms

We implement the following security mechanisms to protect your sensitive Google data:

  • Encryption in Transit (HTTPS/TLS): All communications between your browser and Google's API services are encrypted using industry-standard TLS (Transport Layer Security).
  • OAuth 2.0 Protocol: We utilize Google's secure OAuth 2.0 authentication. We never ask for, see, or store your Google password. We only use temporary, scoped access tokens that reside locally on your device during your active session.
  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: By relying entirely on Google's infrastructure (`drive.appdata`), your sensitive data is protected by Google's own world-class security mechanisms. Human developers of Expire.vip have no access to your stored files or calendar events.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy

Expire.vip's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data is stored in your own Google account, you have full control over it. You can delete your items directly within the Expire.vip app. Additionally, you can revoke Expire.vip's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting your Google Account Security settings (myaccount.google.com/permissions). Revoking access immediately stops all data synchronization and calendar updates.
