Privacy Policy

Expire.vip

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Expire.vip. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. Expire.vip is a "serverless" application. We do not have backend databases, and we do not store, read, or sell your personal data.

2. What Information We Access

When you sign in with Google, we request access to the following information via Google APIs:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your email address to authenticate your session.
  • Google Drive AppData (drive.appdata): We use a hidden, secure folder in your Google Drive to save your list of expiring items. This allows you to sync your items across multiple devices.
  • Google Calendar Events (calendar.events): We request access to create, view, and delete calendar events specifically created by Expire.vip to provide you with automated expiration reminders.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the requested information exclusively to provide the core functionality of the app. Specifically:

  • To safely store your expiration items in your own personal cloud (Google Drive).
  • To insert reminder events into your Google Calendar so you do not miss expiration dates.

We do not use your data for advertising, we do not sell your data, and human developers cannot access your data.

4. Google API Services User Data Policy

Expire.vip's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data is stored in your own Google account, you have full control over it. You can delete your items directly within the Expire.vip app. Additionally, you can revoke Expire.vip's access to your Google Account at any time by visiting your Google Account Security settings (myaccount.google.com/permissions). Revoking access immediately stops all data synchronization and calendar updates.
